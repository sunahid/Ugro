About BloomBox

21st century is all about development and sustainablility. By creating "BloomBox" our goal is to not only promote healthy lifestyles but also help stablizse canada's food insecurityGeorgia Italic is a delicate font and takes insecurity.

Canada is one of the most developed countries with it having the world's 11th largest economy. However, there are still people who suffer from starvation due to lack of money. Food insecurity is one hte issues Canada is facing as certain cohort of our population is not getting enough quantity of food or necessary nutritions.

Why BloomBox?

"BloomBox" is a vertical gardening tool designed to ensure people who cannot afford to buy food from grocery stores everyday have access to fresh fruits and vegetables through self-sufficient agriculture. By using our product, there would be less fruits and vegetables being wasted due to physical imperfection that may not satisfy most of the market's demands. As well, there would not be any problem regarding the transportation of those crops since the consumers can directly get them from their BloomBox. Lastly, it reduces the land usage for farming and increases the efficiency in the production as more crops can be produced with lesser land usage.
